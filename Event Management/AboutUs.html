About Posh Peony Events

For more than 25 years , we have been blessed to be a part of thousands of weddings, debuts, kids parties, corporate events, and private celebrations. In all these events, we always make sure that we are giving are best shot, more importantly at being your partner in every step from conceptualizing, budgeting and planning up to final execution.

WE THRIVE ON MAKING EVENTS THAT ARE TRULY AMAZING

PPE is a living legend, a name synonymous with delightfully innovative events and impeccable service. Our goal is to help you create an incomparable experience by going above and beyond through our service, and styling that is tailored to your taste and budget without scrimping on quality.


The history of the company that became Posh Peony Event stretches back almost as far as the event planning industry in the Philippines itself. Throughout its twenty five plus years existence in the event planning industry, our company has seen various milestones both internally and within the industry. All of these milestones have made us the leading event planning and consultancy service in the country today.

MISSION

VISION

Our mission at Posh Peony Events is to provide excellent quality service to clients at value-for money prices and personalized manner as performed by a highly trained and cohesive team of experienced professionals. Our main focus shall and always will be to assist clients in achieving their dream of a perfect event while maintaining the company’s ideals and integrity.

Our vision at Posh Peony Event is to continue our pioneering efforts in the event planning industry. We will continue to expand beyond the country’s borders and create a presence in the global market place. The company also aims to exert all its resources in professionalizing the wedding coordination industry through proper and intensive training methods.
